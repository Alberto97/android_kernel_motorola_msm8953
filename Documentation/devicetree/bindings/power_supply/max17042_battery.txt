max17042_battery
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Required properties :
 - compatible : "maxim,max17042"

Optional properties :
 - maxim,rsns-microohm : Resistance of rsns resistor in micro Ohms
                         (datasheet-recommended value is 10000).
   Defining this property enables current-sense functionality.
